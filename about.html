Welcome to Caravan Afghanistan

Caravan Afghanistan Travel and Tour is a registered and trusted travel agency under the Afghan Tour Organization, Ministry of Information and Culture. Based in Kabul, Afghanistan, we specialize in providing authentic, safe, and unforgettable travel experiences across the country.

Whether you’re an adventurer, history enthusiast, or cultural explorer, our all-in-one travel services ensure a seamless journey with expert guidance, comfortable transport, and personalized itineraries.
